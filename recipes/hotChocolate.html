Hot Chocolate

Description

A Crock-Pot Hot Chocolate that only takes 5 ingredients to produce and couldn't be easier to make! You simply combine all the ingredients into the Crock-Pot and let it simmer!

Ingredients

  • 6 Cups Milk
  • 2 Cups Semisweet Chocolate Chips
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. 1. Combine milk, chocolate chips, cream, condensed milk, and vanilla in a slow cooker.
  2. 2. Cover and cook on Low, stirring occasionally, until chocolate chips are melted and mixture is smooth, about 2 hours.
  3. 3. Serve!
